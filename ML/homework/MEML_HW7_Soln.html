Problem 3¶

Look at the following training set for a decision tree that classifies names as either location or person names:


1) Calculate disorder of attribute 1.

2) Calculate disorder of attribute 2.

3) Which attribute is more useful, attribute 1 or attribute 2 Why?

Solution 3¶

$$\begin{align*} D & = -\frac{l}{T} \log_2 \frac{l}{T} - \frac{p}{T} \log_2 \frac{p}{T} \qquad \text{where }\; l: \text{location}, \; p: \text{person},\; T: \text{Total}\\ \\ \end{align*} $$$$Q(\text{test}) = \sum D(\text{set}) \times \frac{\text{# of samples in set}}{\text{# of samples in all sets}}$$

1) Calculate disorder of attribute 1.

$$\begin{align*} D_{\text{attribute1 yes}} & = -\frac{3}{6} \log_2 \frac{3}{6} - \frac{3}{6} \log_2 \frac{3}{6} = 1\\ \\ D_{\text{attribute1 no}} & = -\frac{1}{3} \log_2 \frac{1}{3} - \frac{2}{3} \log_2 \frac{2}{3} = 0.9183 \end{align*} $$

2) Calculate disorder of attribute 2.

$$\begin{align*} D_{\text{attribute2 yes}} & = -\frac{4}{4} \log_2 \frac{4}{4} - \frac{0}{4} \log_2 \frac{0}{4} = 0\\ \\ D_{\text{attribute2 no}} & = -\frac{0}{5} \log_2 \frac{0}{5} - \frac{5}{5} \log_2 \frac{5}{5} = 0 \end{align*} $$


3) Which attribute is more useful, attribute 1 or attribute 2 Why?

$$ \begin{align*} Q_{\text{attribute1}} &= 1\times\frac{6}{9} + 0.9183\times\frac{3}{9} = 0.973\\ Q_{\text{attribute2}} &= 0\times\frac{4}{9} + 0\times\frac{5}{9} = 0\\\\ Q_{\text{attribute1}} &> Q_{\text{attribute2}}\\\\ \therefore \;&\text{attribute 2 is more useful.} \end{align*} $$
